    Big thanks to AbertayHackers


    I was able to attend LTDH22 thanks to @AbertayHackers. So shout out to them for giving me the opportunity.

    Really enjoyed talks from:




  • @Tiaaa_C - Practicing Safe Sex(t). I've tried many times to see Tia's talk but always seemed to miss it. When I seen she was going to present it one final time at Le Tour Du Hack I made it my mission to not miss it this time. I really enjoyed the talk and was very well presented. Very thought-provoking stuff was shown.
  • @PyroGuy_UK - Cyber Security Community in Scotland + 3 key things I wish I had known 20 years ago. The message for this talk was brilliant. The cyber security community is very close and this talk highlighted it. To remind attendees that we should continue to engage with one another and even go further in the future. It was a really nice talk to start of the conference.
  • @Cyb34_DO - An unexpected Journey into Cybersecurity. Very informative talk on the role of a security analyst. Various portals were discussed including Azure Sentinel, Defender for Endpoint, Identity and Cloud. Took a lot away from this talk.
  • @hopkira - Quantum Computing. Finally, this was a fun closing keynote. Admittedly I missed quite a lot of this talk. However from what I was able to see it was mad fascinating. I heard some algorithms being mentioned which is a topic I'm interested in. Looking forward to seeing the recording for what I missed in it.

    • Again thanks to @AbertayHackers :).
